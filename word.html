babayē

Dialect:

Rinconada

antonym

lalakē

Pronounciation

  • "Ba" sounds like "bah" with a short "a" sound.
  • "Bay" sounds like "bahy," with a long "a" sound and a short "i" sound.
  • "Yi" sounds like "yee," with a long "i" sound.

So, altogether, it's "ba-bah-yee."

examples

Tagalog:Magandang babae.


English:Beautiful woman.

Tagalog:naglaba yung babae kanina.


English:The woman did the laundry earlier.

Tagalog Meaning

Ang salitang "babae" ay tumutukoy sa kasarian ng tao na karaniwang tinutukoy bilang "female" sa Ingles.

English Meaning

The term "woman" refers to an adult human female. It is typically used to describe individuals who have reached maturity and belong to the female gender.
